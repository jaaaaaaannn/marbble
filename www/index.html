Concept

Marbble is a free multilingual word game, the first of its kind! Yes, you read that right: multilingual. That means an english player can play in English against a french player that plays in French! To play, you have to make words on the board using the letters on your rack. You can optimize your score with special cells («Double Letter», «Triple Word»…) and by using high values letter tiles to make rare words on your own… or reusing the words made by your opponent.

We want Marbble to be a fun and educational game. That’s why you’ll also have access to the translations and definitions of the words played during a game. We hope that it will help you to enrich your vocabulary and to learn new languages.

When people share the same language, they become brothers. That’s why we developed Marbble. We want to gather people all over the world, we want them to make one, we want Universal Peace!

Ok this might sound overdone, but you get the idea.

Features

The current version is 1.0.4

Here are the current features available and the ones we’d like to implement in the future:

  • Game modes

    • Marbble: a multilingual mode where players use their own bag of tiles adapted to their language.
    • Classic: a classic monolingual word game where players share the same bag of tiles.
    • Open & Collaborative (under development): an open mode where you can play monolingual and multilingual games. In this mode all words are accepted, even if they’re not found in the game dictionaries. Why collaborative ? Because all words played in this mode can be submitted to a peer-review system and then be added to the game dictionaries if they’re validated by the Marbble community. You can enrich and participate to Marbble improvement by simply playing it !

  • Gameplay

    • Pass & Play mode: Players pass the device around after playing their turn.
    • Solo game: Play against computer with different levels of difficulty
    • Play with a Facebook friend: Play with your Facebook friends
    • Find me an opponent: play against a random opponent thanks to a clever matchmaking engine

  • Languages:

    • English
    • French
    • Spanish
    • Filipino
    • German
    • Italian
    • Portuguese

  • Definitions and translations

    • In-game translations
    • In-game definitions

  • Other Features

    • Possibility to play several games at a time
    • In-game documentation
    • Statistics, customization of players profiles, in-game challenges …
    • Upgrade of design with smooth animations
