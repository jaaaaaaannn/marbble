RAIN = LLOVER

English
Spanish
noun:
1. water that is condensed from the aqueous vapor in the atmospher and falls to earth in drops more than 1/50 (0.5mm) in diameter. Compare drizzle (def 6).
2. a rainfall, rainstorm, or shower: We had a light rain this afternoon.
3. rains, rainy season, seasonal rainfall, as in India.
